## 1. Initial Request & Workspace Analysis
The conversation started with a request to analyse the entire workspace and to completely learn the localized `printora_slc_strategy.md.resolved` strategy document.

### Technical & Strategic Summary
*   **The Strategy (The SLC Funnel):** The strategy identified that creating graphics is the #1 friction point for customers. To solve this, Printora uses a three-tier "Sell Like Crazy" offer strategy:
    *   **Offer 1 (€15 Consulenza Grafica):** An affordable, expert-driven graphic design/correction service with a 24h turnaround for warm traffic.
    *   **Offer 2 (Guida Editor):** A free 5-step High-Value Content Offer (HVCO) teaching beginners how to use the online editor.
    *   **Offer 3 (Checklist Stampa):** A free 7-point technical HVCO for agencies to verify CMYK, bleed, and DPI settings.
*   **Workspace Validation:** Analysis of `printora-frontend` and `printora-backend` confirmed that the required UI structures (React pages and components) such as `GraficaConsultingPage.jsx`, `GuidaEditorPage.jsx`, and `GuidaFilePage.jsx`, as well as the related routing in `App.jsx`, are actively implemented.
